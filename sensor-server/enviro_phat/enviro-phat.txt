BMP 280 Temp/Pressure Sensor
   Req/Rep messages:

     One Shot data:
        SENSOR_REQ,DEV=ENVIRO_PHAT,SUB_DEV=BMP,CMD=READ,SENSOR_REQ_END
        SENSOR_REP,DEV=ENVIRO_PHAT,SUB_DEV=BMP,TEMP=23.3,PRES=10000.00,ALT=132.33,SENSOR_REP_END

     Subscribe to data stream:
        SENSOR_REQ,DEV=ENVIRO_PHAT,SUB_DEV=BMP,CMD=SUB_START,RATE=1000,SENSOR_REQ_END
        SENSOR_REP,DEV=ENVIRO_PHAT,SUB_DEV=BMP,STATUS=OK|BUSY,SENSOR_REP_END

     Stop subscription:
        SENSOR_REQ,DEV=ENVIRO_PHAT,SUB_DEV=BMP,CMD=SUB_STOP,SENSOR_REQ_END
        SENSOR_REP,DEV=ENVIRO_PHAT,SUB_DEV=BMP,STATUS=OK,SENSOR_REP_END

     Pub messages:
        SENSOR_PUB,DEV=ENVIRO_PHAT,SUB_DEV=BMP,TEMP=23.3,PRES=10000.00,ALT=132.33,SENSOR_PUB_END
